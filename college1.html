About IIT Bombay

The Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay) is a public technical and research university located in Powai, Mumbai. Established in 1958 with assistance from UNESCO and the Soviet Union, it is the second-oldest institute of the Indian Institutes of Technology system.

Recognized as an Institute of Eminence by the Government of India, IIT Bombay is known for its outstanding academic programs, cutting-edge research facilities, and distinguished faculty. The institute has consistently ranked among the top engineering institutes in India and has produced numerous notable alumni who have made significant contributions in various fields globally.

IIT Bombay's sprawling 550-acre campus is situated in the serene surroundings of Powai Lake, providing an ideal environment for academic pursuits and research excellence.

Admission Process

Admissions to IIT Bombay are highly competitive. Here's how you can apply:

1

JEE Advanced (Undergraduate)

Admissions to B.Tech programs are based on JEE Advanced ranks and through the Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) counseling process.

2

GATE (Postgraduate)

M.Tech admissions are primarily through the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) followed by interviews.

3

CEED (Design Programs)

For design programs, candidates must qualify through the Common Entrance Examination for Design (CEED).

4

JAM (M.Sc Programs)

For M.Sc programs, Joint Admission Test for M.Sc (JAM) is the qualifying examination.
